DINESENKO LYUDMILA ALEXANDROVNA OR SIMPLY LUSYA WAS BORN ON OCTOBER 6TH, 1932 IN ALTAI KRAI, RUSSIA. LUSYA’S PARENTS PASSED AWAY WHEN SHE WAS ONLY THREE YEARS OLD, SO LUSYA WAS RAISED BY HER GRANDMOTHER TATIANA NIKOLAYEVNA. ON LUCKY DAYS LUSYA WAS ABLE TO TRAVEL TO HER GRANDFATHER DMITRYI, WHERE HER OLDER BROTHER GENA LIVED. GROWING UP DURING THE SECOND WORLD WAR BROUGHT MANY HARDSHIPS, BUT IT NEVER STOPPED LUSYA TO PUT HER EDUCATION FIRST. THROUGH HARD WORK SHE GOT A PLACE AT THE UNIVERSITY OF MOSCOW, WHERE SHE MET MY GRANDFATHER VOVA AND FELL IN LOVE.
