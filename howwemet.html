Dinesenko Lyudmila Alexandrovna or simply Lusya. Lusya was born on October 6th, 1932, in a small village city called Glubokoe in the Altar Krai in Russia. Lusya’s parents passed away when Lusya was only three years old, and her grandmother Tanya. Lysya’s childhood was not easy, growing up during the Second World War with extreme famine and poverty. Nevertheless, because of her dedication and hard work Lusya managed to score a place at Moscow State University.
